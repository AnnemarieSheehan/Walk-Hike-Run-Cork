See the very best of Cork on foot!

Cork has some of Ireland's finest coastline and natural beauty so make the most of your visit, put on your hikng boots or runners and get outdoors.

Seaside towns, rugged cliff walks and green overgrown paths make the Rebel County the perfect spot to stretch your legs.

We give you reviews of some of the more popular places as well as a hidden gem or two! Walking, hiking or running are all excellent ways to see the surrounding countryside - Enjoy!
